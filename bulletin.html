Review - AFCM 25ths Anniversary Concert

December 31, 2016
A very pleasant Sunday afternoon in Queens Gardens was attended by approximately three and half thousand satisfied music lovers. This free concert organised to celebrate the 25th Anniversary of the AFCM was also a fitting opportunity for BRO to celebrate it's 15th Aniiversary with it's first ever outdoor public performance.

Review - Dreams and Dances

December 31, 2017
Dance and music go together likee a, ahem, horse and carriage or in 2016 a iPhone and a thumb! But unlike the old song you certainly can have one without the other and the Barrier Reef Orchestra's first concert for the year Dreams and Dances definitely proved that.

Review - World Music Concert

December 31, 2016
The World Music Concert was a breath of fresh air to Townsville music lovers who has just about reached classical music saturation following AFCM. Andrew Ryder poured his expertise and enthusiasm into this opportunity proved though Townsville City Council sponsorship.

President's Report - 2016

December 31, 2016
2015 was our 15 year anniversary and featured several concerts that highlighted not only the enhanced quality of the musical performance from the orchestra but an increasing recognition from our audience, benefactors and the wider community.

Review - T150 Concert

October 20, 2014
Those among us in the capacity audience in the Civic theatre to celebrate Townsville's 150th anniversary had a real treat of a birthday present. The Variety concert would have had about 97% Townsville connected content and showcased what talent is in Townsville.
